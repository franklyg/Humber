About Manic to Panic

From the enchanted forests' all the way to the undiscovered roads lies the members of Manic to Panic. All the way from Oak Ridges, Ontario is where the band meets everyday to create the music they love to create. Originating in 2010 as the band known as, YORK original members Josh and Franky managed to settle their differences after their break up and create what is now Manic to Panic. Not yet exposed to the music scene, they hope to eventually get plenty of shows and release music to build a much bigger fan base.
